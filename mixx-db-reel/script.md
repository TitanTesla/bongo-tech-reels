**Rules:** pure-black canvas · no gradients · hand-drawn SVG only · TZS everywhere · Gen-Z Swahili/English code-switching · tech terms stay in English (database, transaction, atomicity, index, row, SQL) · everything inside TikTok/Reels safe zones · no bank framing anywhere · script stays 100% inside the Tigo Pesa / Mixx by Yas world — no other apps mentioned · accents: Mixx yellow + Tigo-heritage blue · emoji moja tu kwenye canvas (🙄 ya Delete) — zingine ni hand-drawn SVG glyphs.

## Scene 2 — BALANCE YAKO SI PESA ILIYOKAA MAHALI (0:07 – 0:19)

**VO:**
> Siri ya kwanza: ile balance unayoiona kwenye Mixx — haimaanishi kuna pesa zako zimekaa mahali fulani zikikusubiri. Hapana. 😅 Hakuna "kachumba ka Frank" kenye TZS 113,500. Kilichopo ni **records** — kila ulichopokea, kila ulichotuma, kama daftari la deni la mangi. Balance ni **jumla** tu ya story hiyo.

**Canvas:** Phone screen inaonyesha `TZS 113,500`. Rows zina-sum live, jibu lina-snap kurudi kwenye screen: `TZS 113,500`. Label: `LEDGER — si pesa, ni story`.

## Scene 3 — ATOMICITY: BILA HII, PESA HEWANI (0:19 – 0:32)

**VO:**
> Siri ya pili: **atomicity**. Hebu fikiria bila hii: unatuma TZS 50,000, network inakufa Ubungo... wewe umekatwa, Mama Neema hajapokea. Pesa iko hewani. 💀 Ndio maana database ina rule: transaction inafanyika **YOTE**, au **haifanyiki kabisa**. Ukikatwa — mwenzako amepokea. Guaranteed. Hakuna nusu-nusu.

**Canvas:** Split screen. Kushoto — `BILA ATOMICITY`: orb ina-toka kwa Frank (`−50,000` ina-flash red), signal bars zina-drop hadi ✕, orb ina-freeze katikati na ku-dissolve... Mama Neema bado `+0`. Skull ndogo ya hand-drawn SVG. Kulia — `NA ATOMICITY`: glitch ile ile, lakini orb ina-**rewind** yenyewe hadi kwa Frank, balance zote mbili zina-flash bila kubadilika. Stamp: `ALL OR NOTHING`.

## Scene 4 — INDEX: BILA HII, FOLENI YA NCHI NZIMA (0:32 – 0:44)

**VO:**
> Siri ya tatu: **index**. Watu zaidi ya millioni 10 kwenye system. Bila index, ukibonyeza *check balance*, database ingesoma account ya kila mtu — mmoja mmoja — jibu lingekuja baada ya masaa. Nchi nzima kwenye foleni. 😭 Na index? Ni kama contacts kwenye simu: huna ku-scroll, una-jump moja kwa moja kwa jina. Jibu — **millisecond**.

**Canvas:** Split screen. Kushoto — `BILA INDEX`: grid ya dots mamillioni, scan line inatambaa row kwa row, stick figures wana-jiunga kwenye foleni inayozidi kurefuka, progress bar: `~4 hrs`. Kulia — `NA INDEX`: index tree ina-draw kwa strokes mbili-tatu, dot moja ina-light instant — `0.001s`. Counter kubwa side-by-side: `4 hrs` vs `0.001s`.

## Scene 5 — DELETE NI UONGO (NA NI MAKUSUDI) (0:44 – 0:54)

**VO:**

Database ina-mark tu: *"usimwonyeshe huyu."* Na ni makusudi — kama records zingefutika kweli, ungetuma TZS 100,000, mpokeaji ana-delete history na kusema *"sijapokea kitu."* Ushahidi umeyeyuka. 👀 Ndio maana history haipotei. Delete ni kujificha... si kufa.

**Canvas:** Row ya transaction (`TZS 100,000 → received`) ina-swipe-delete — lakini badala ya kupotea, ina-slide chini ya curtain na flag: `hidden = true`. Cut: chat bubble ya mpokeaji: *"sijapokea kitu 🙄"* — curtain ina-funguka kidogo, ile row imefifia ina-rise kama receipt. Caption ndogo: `records hazidanganyi`.

## Quick reference — the 4 surprising facts (each with its "bila hii" contrast)

1. **Hakuna pesa inayosafiri** — transfer ni database ku-edit rows mbili (hook fact).
2. **Balance yako si pesa iliyokaa mahali** — hakuna "kachumba" chenye jina lako; ni jumla ya ledger records.
3. **Atomicity** — *bila hii:* umekatwa, mwenzako hajapokea, pesa hewani. *Na hii:* yote au hakuna.
4. **Index** — *bila hii:* full scan ya watu millioni 10+, jibu baada ya masaa, foleni ya nchi nzima. *Na hii:* millisecond.
5. **Delete si delete** — *kama ingekuwa kweli:* mpokeaji anafuta history, ushahidi unayeyuka. *Ndio maana:* soft delete — `hidden`, bado ipo.

Bonus: **SQL ni ya 1974** — ina umri mkubwa kuliko wazazi wa wengi wanao-scroll hii reel.
